Xpath by Contains

-It has more advantage than anyOther xpaths.
-It is used most of the time in market.
-When we have to pass partial Attribute or Partial Text value we use Xpath by Contains

Syntax- 	//tagname[contains(text(),'Partial Text value')] ----------- For Text

                	//tagname[contains(@Attribute name,'Partial Attribute value')]-----For Attribute
